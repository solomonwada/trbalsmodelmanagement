Welcome To The Tribe

Who We Are

Tribals Model Management is an Afrocentric modeling/art house established in 2020. It is an agency that brings the world a platform to promote the African culture in all its entirety by creating a space for young models/creatives to express themselves through fashion.

Why We Are Here

We stand as a lens that projects African art through photography, body art, cinematography etc. We are also in the business of advertising and model promotions for all your advertising needs.

Our Goal

Our ultimate goal is to ensure the envisioned passion of our young aspiring models/creatives become a reality by achieving their career dreams, not leaving the fun attached.

The C.E.O

Theo Kennedy

Theophilus Eromonsele Omosun popularly know as Theo Kennedy is the CEO of Tribals Model Management, a graduate of Architecture from the university of Ilorin, Ilorin Kwara state, Nigeria, he hails from Edo state Nigeria, He is a Serial Entrepreneur and believes that the sky is just the starting point for anyone and you can achieve whatever you set your mind to do.

He created Tribals Model Management and the Tribals brand for the sole purpose of creating a platform for Models that want to model to express themselves in the art of modeling.

It is also a platform for Creatives to show case and Express themselves in their Arts and Craft and what they do best, He says everyone is invited to join Tribals and be the ones responsible for bringing the world to Africa.
